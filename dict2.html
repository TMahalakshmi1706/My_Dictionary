Palimpsest


Definition: A manuscript or piece of writing material on which later writing has been superimposed on effaced earlier writing.

Usage in Sentences:

  • The city's architecture was a palimpsest of historical eras.
  • Her memories felt like a palimpsest, layered and fading.
  • His life was a palimpsest of past identities and changes.
  • The old walls were a visual palimpsest, holding stories beneath the paint.
  • The building was renovated but remained a palimpsest of colonial influence.
Synonyms Antonyms
Manuscript Original
Layered text Fresh start
Rewritten document Blank slate
Rewrite Clean copy
