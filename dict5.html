Zephyr


Definition: A gentle, mild breeze.

Usage in Sentences:

  • The zephyr rustled the leaves in the trees.
  • She felt a zephyr of cool air on her face.
  • The zephyr carried the scent of blooming flowers.
  • The zephyr whispered through the open window.
  • As the sun set, a gentle zephyr filled the air.
