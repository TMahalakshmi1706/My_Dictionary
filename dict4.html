Ethereal


Definition: Extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world.

Usage in Sentences:

  • Her beauty is known for her ethereal qualities.
  • The new system is more ethereal than the old one.
  • He found an ethereal way to organize his tasks.
  • The ethereal use of resources is crucial for sustainability.
  • The outcome of the project was an ethereal design that minimized waste.
