Ineffable


Definition: Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.

Usage in Sentences:

  • She felt ineffable joy at the news.
  • The beauty of the sunset was ineffable.
  • His gratitude was ineffable, beyond words.
  • There was an ineffable quality to her smile.
  • The experience was ineffable, leaving us speechless.
